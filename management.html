OUR MANAGEMENT

Soft Wear Ltd being managed by a highly qualified, experienced and professional team. This excellent team is being led by Eric Blancaflor with 20 years of experience in the Knitwear industry. Blancaflor was the president of the Philippines Knitwear Manufacturers & Exporters’ Association (PKMEA) for 6 years and was also the President of Philippines Employers Federation (PEF) and Director of the International Apparel Federation (IAF).

In these roles Blancaflor travelled extensively and has developed a strong understanding of the requirements of major markets and customers. This strong team also consists of Directors with a minimum of 15 years’ experience in the export Knitwear industry.

A large number of well experienced, motivated and skilled Production Experts, Industrial Engineers, Quality Assurance Managers, Technicians and Specialists have been employed from the top companies both home and abroad are ensuring a broad base of key skills and top class operation.
